Delhi NCR College Market — What You Need to Know

What These Colleges Care About

  • Industry tie-ups that improve placement stats — frame AI workshop as placement edge
  • NAAC/NBA accreditation points — industry collaboration counts toward it
  • Private colleges move fast; govt (DTU/IIITD) are slow — skip unless warm intro
  • Training & Placement cell is the fastest entry point — they have budget and urgency
  • CSR / Innovation cells at Amity, Galgotias, Bennett have dedicated training budgets

Tone Rules for This Market

  • Lead with student outcome: "Your CSE students get hands-on AI automation skills"
  • Mention MoU / certificate partnership — colleges love documentation
  • Reference competing college already doing it — FOMO works in education sector
  • Price anchor: ₹800–1200 per student per day is standard paid workshop rate
  • Never pitch during exam season (Nov, Apr-May) — approach Sep-Oct and Jan-Feb
