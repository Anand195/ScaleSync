Bhopal College Market — What You Need to Know

What These Colleges Care About

  • RGPV affiliation dominates private colleges — T&P and HOD have fastest budget authority
  • MoU-backed industry workshops carry serious weight for NAAC/NBA accreditation — always lead with documentation angle
  • TIT Bhopal and RNTU already have Google/AWS/IBM MoUs — frame ScaleSync as additive, not competing
  • LNCT is the scale play — group campuses means one yes could be 3–5 workshops
  • Bhopal colleges respond to central India framing — "MP's first AI automation workshop series"

Tone Rules for This Market

  • ScaleSync is Ahmedabad-based — position as "expanding to central India, Bhopal first" not outsider
  • Mention rupee amounts upfront — ₹800–1000/student standard; budget-conscious market
  • Free pilot removes friction for first contact — especially effective in RGPV-affiliated private colleges
  • Avoid exam windows (Nov, Apr–May); best contact windows are Aug–Oct and Jan–Mar
  • Phone outreach stronger than Ahmedabad — Bhopal decision makers respond to direct calls
