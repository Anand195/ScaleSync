The Selling Framework for This Market

The 5 Psychological Levers to Pull

  • AICTE Mandate — External authority. They don't have a choice. Frame you as the solution to a problem they already have.
  • Fear of Falling Behind — "Two colleges across the city are already in our June cohort. Your students will interview against theirs."
  • Loss Aversion — Students graduating without AI skills face 40% lower shortlisting rates at Bengaluru/Pune product companies. That's the HOD's failure, not the student's.
  • Social Proof — IIT Guwahati. Multiple institutions. Name it every time. It changes their brain's trust calculation instantly.
  • Scarcity — Only 3 onboarding slots this month. Say it. Mean it. Then mean it harder.

Hormozi Value Stack — Use This Before Quoting Price

  • Lead with: "Here's everything you get..." then list all components with their standalone value
  • 2-Day Workshop alone: curriculum delivery (₹50k value), industry certificates, MoU documentation, AICTE compliance, GitHub portfolios, post-workshop support
  • After stacking: "We charge ₹900/student. That's it."
  • Free pilot = risk reversal. "You don't pay until you've seen the quality." Nobody says no to this.
  • Local angle works hard here: "We're Ahmedabad-based. If anything goes wrong, we're 20 minutes away." Trust closer.
  • Always end with: "What would have to be true for you to say yes today?"
